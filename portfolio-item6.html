A website to predict product demand using Bayes Theorem

A web application that predicts product demand by using classification rules and Bayes Theorem for probability and statistics. The iterative waterfall model was used for development. The website used C#, ASP.NET 4.0 and Visual Studio for front end development and MS SQL server for backend.Proposed system is a web enabled application which helps the business to know the product demand before manufacturing. Here for the prediction of product demand we make use of data mining technique called as classification rules. Proposed system is an automated system that predicts the demand of the product. The proposed system helps the business to get the best profit. By using this system the business entity can avoid the manufacturing of less demand products. The system uses Na√Øve-Bayes theorem and classification rules to predict the demand of the product by going through historical data and sales data of the company. This prediction mechanism will help the company determine which products will be a success in current market. In this case we are considering mobile phones, and the proposed system will determine the desirable features customers expect from their phones. And by considering these features the system determines whether to produce the new product or not. The proposed system helps the company to sell its goods more efficiently. This system helps to sell 80-85% of its produced goods, compared to 50-55% sales of the system that does not use product demand forecasting. Hence by using the proposed system the company sales will increase up to 30-35% from the previous sales record and it also reduces the cost of production considerably.
